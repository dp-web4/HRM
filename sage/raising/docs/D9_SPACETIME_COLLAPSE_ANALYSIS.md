## Theoretical Interpretation

### Why D9 (Spacetime) Collapse?

D9 represents temporal/spatial context - the "where and when" of information. In this failure mode:

1. **Self-generated content** gets stronger temporal anchoring than externally-provided content
2. **Context clearing** fails to remove self-generated content because it's part of the "self" state
3. **Retrieval** preferentially accesses self-generated content due to stronger activation

### Possible Mechanisms

1. **Self-reference loops**: Content generated by the model activates more strongly on retrieval
2. **Temporal ordering confusion**: Self-generated content tagged as "earlier" despite appearing later
3. **Attention asymmetry**: Model attends more to its own productions than external inputs
4. **KV-cache contamination**: Self-generated tokens persist in key-value representations
